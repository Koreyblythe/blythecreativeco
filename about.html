About Blythe Creative

**Blythe Creative** is a marketing agency founded by **Korey Blythe**, a **Business Administration graduate from John A. Logan College**, currently studying **Marketing at Southern Illinois University Carbondale (SIU)**.

Korey’s mission is to help **small and local businesses** transform their **branding, marketing, and social media presence** to stand out and grow.

Brand Identity

Creating unique logos, visual identities, and branding strategies to make businesses instantly recognizable.

Marketing Strategy

Helping businesses develop and execute marketing plans tailored to their audience and growth goals.

Social Media Management

Providing expert guidance and content strategies to increase engagement and drive business through social media.

Consulting

Offering insights and coaching on branding, customer engagement, and business development.
