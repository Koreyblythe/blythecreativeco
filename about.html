Meet the Founder

Korey Blythe is the driving force behind Blythe Creative. With a **Business Administration degree from John A. Logan College** and ongoing **Marketing studies at Southern Illinois University Carbondale (SIU)**, Korey brings a dynamic blend of expertise and passion to the industry.

His vision extends beyond traditional marketing—Korey is committed to empowering **small and local businesses** by transforming their branding, digital presence, and strategic growth efforts. By combining creative innovation with data-driven marketing strategies, Blythe Creative helps businesses establish a lasting impact in their industry.
